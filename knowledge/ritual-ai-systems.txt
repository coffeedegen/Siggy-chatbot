Why does AI need to be on-chain? Today's developers have unprecedented access to AI tools for creating responsive, tailored user experiences. At the same time, maintaining AI systems that are transparent, unbiased, and free from undue control has become more crucial than ever. Ritual sits at the intersection of Crypto and AI.

Model inference is the process of using a trained AI model to generate outputs from new inputs. Ritual enables verifiable model training with cryptographic guarantees of correctness and provenance. An enshrined model is an AI model that is natively represented on-chain as a first-class citizen, enabling direct smart contract integration and verifiable execution.

Ritual supports multiple verification approaches. ZKML combines zero-knowledge proofs with machine learning to verify model computations without revealing inputs, weights, or intermediate values. OPML assumes computations are correct by default but allows challenge periods. PPML uses statistical properties and backdoors in ML models to prove training authenticity. Modular computational integrity lets developers choose and combine ZKML, OPML, and PPML based on their needs.

The Model Marketplace is a decentralized platform for trading AI models with built-in provenance tracking, licensing, and automated revenue distribution. The Model Registry handles model versioning, ownership, and provenance verification on-chain. Infernet is a decentralized oracle network for bringing verified AI computation on-chain, with secure model execution, result verification, and cross-chain integration. The Agent Launchpad is a platform for deploying and managing autonomous agents with built-in economic incentives and security guarantees.

Modular storage supports multiple backends including web2 (HuggingFace) and web3 (Arweave). Native account abstraction fundamentally expands the capabilities of EOA accounts for enhanced UX. Ritual is the best venue for developers harnessing AI, ZK, or TEEs in their applications.
