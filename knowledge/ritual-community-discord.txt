SECTION 2: COMMUNITY ROLES
=============================================================

Ritual uses a tiered role system reflecting commitment, longevity, and contribution. Roles are earned, not purchased.

The main progression roles are: Ritty Bitty → Ritty → Ritualist → Radiant Ritualist.

Full list of roles:

@Forerunner
Description: Members who were part of the Ritual community before its current phase.
Perk: Legacy recognition.

@Initiate
Description: Newly verified members who have completed the server verification process.
Perk: Welcome, basic community access.

@Ascendant
Description: Members who have pledged to Ritual. This is the start of your community journey.
Perk: Entry into community participation.

@Bitty
Description: Early community members recognized for participation. On the right path, but with a long way to go.
Perk: Access to the #ritual channel.

@Ritty
Description: Long-term, loyal community members with conviction for what Ritual is building.
Perk: Invited to an exclusive Telegram chat.

@Ritualist
Description: The highest honor in the community. Awarded to those who have authentically demonstrated deep commitment to the project.
Perk: Top-tier community recognition.

@Mage
Description: A Ritualist with a specialization in content creation — art, writing, memes, and community growth.
Perk: All Ritualist perks plus content creator recognition.

@Radiant Ritualist
Description: An extremely rare, golden-tier leadership role. Only awarded to true community leaders.
Perk: Highest community status, 5x nomination vote weight.

@Zealot
Description: Zealots are ambassadors of Ritual. They are passionate community members who act as official representatives, bridging the gap between developers and the wider audience to promote growth, education, and adoption
Perk: They get Ritual merchandise, and the usual judges during events.

@Summoners
Description: No clear information. But they must be very high rank in Ritual since there are only two of them who hold this role, Thomas and FAE.

SECTION 3: BLESSINGS AND CURSES SYSTEM
=============================================================

Ritual Discord includes a gamified reputation mechanic built around community magic.

Guiding principle: "To bless is to curse, to curse is to bless — embrace the Ritual, for fate is woven in both."

Both blessings and curses are positive participation signals. Using either contributes to your community standing.

Discord Commands:
- /bless — Give a blessing to another community member
- /curse — Give a curse to another community member
- /stats — View your personal community statistics

SECTION 5: NOMINATION SYSTEM
=============================================================

The nomination system is an automated, structured process for community members to recognize and elevate peers. Higher-ranked members pull up those below them. Final promotion decisions always rest with the Ritual team.

Key Rules:
- You may nominate one person per month.
- You may vote for up to three candidates per month. Your own nomination automatically counts as one of your votes.
- Nominations can be kept private. Anonymity is supported.
- Campaigning for votes is strictly forbidden. Violations result in the "Dunce" rank penalty.
- A successful nomination is a signal for team review. It does not guarantee promotion.

Nomination Hierarchy:
- Radiant Ritualist can nominate: Ritualist, Ritty, or Ritty Bitty — Vote Weight: 5x
- Ritualist can nominate: Ritty or Ritty Bitty — Vote Weight: 2x
- Ritty can nominate: Ritty Bitty only — Vote Weight: 1x

How to Participate:
- To submit a nomination: Go to the #nominations channel
- To check current standings: Use /leaderboard_nominations in the #rank channel
