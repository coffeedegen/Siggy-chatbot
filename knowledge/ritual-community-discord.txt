3. Blessings & Curses System

Ritual Discord includes a gamified reputation mechanic built around
community magic. The guiding principle:

  "To bless is to curse, to curse is to bless — embrace the Ritual, for
  fate is woven in both."

Both blessings and curses are positive participation signals. Using
either contributes to your community standing.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Command         Action
  --------------- -------------------------------------------------------
  /bless          Give a blessing to another community member

  /curse          Give a curse to another community member

  /stats          View your personal community statistics
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Nomination System

The nomination system is an automated, structured process for community
members to recognize and elevate peers. Higher-ranked members "pull up"
those below them. Final promotion decisions always rest with the Ritual
team.

5.1 Key Rules

-   You may nominate one person per month.

-   You may vote for up to three candidates per month. Your own
    nomination automatically counts as one of your votes.

-   Nominations can be kept private (anonymity is supported).

-   Campaigning for votes is strictly forbidden — violations result in
    the "Dunce" rank penalty.

-   A successful nomination is a signal for team review — it does not
    guarantee promotion.

5.2 Nomination Hierarchy

The system follows a strict rank-based hierarchy governing who can
nominate whom and with what voting weight:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Your Rank               Who You Can Nominate    Vote Weight
  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------
  Radiant Ritualist       Ritualist, Ritty, or    5x
                          Ritty Bitty             

  Ritualist               Ritty or Ritty Bitty    2x

  Ritty                   Ritty Bitty only        1x
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
