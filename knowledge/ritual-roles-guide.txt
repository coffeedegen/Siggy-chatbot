RITUAL COMMUNITY ROLES GUIDE

=============================================================
SECTION 1: ROLE OVERVIEW
=============================================================

Ritual uses a tiered role system reflecting commitment, longevity, and contribution.
Roles are earned, not purchased.

The main progression path is: Bitty → Ritty → Ritualist → Radiant Ritualist.

=============================================================
SECTION 2: FULL LIST OF ROLES
=============================================================

@Forerunner
Description: Members who were part of the Ritual community before its current phase.
Perk: Legacy recognition.

@Initiate
Description: Newly verified members who have completed the server verification process.
Perk: Welcome, basic community access.

@Ascendant
Description: Members who have pledged to Ritual. This is the start of your community journey.
Perk: Entry into community participation.

@Bitty
Also known as: Ritty Bitty
Description: Early community members recognized for participation. On the right path, but with a long way to go.
Perk: Access to the #ritual channel.

@Ritty
Description: Long-term, loyal community members with conviction for what Ritual is building.
Perk: Invited to an exclusive Telegram chat.

@Ritualist
Description: The highest honor in the community. Awarded to those who have authentically demonstrated deep commitment to the project.
Perk: Top-tier community recognition.

@Mage
Description: A Ritualist with a specialization in content creation — art, writing, memes, and community growth.
Perk: All Ritualist perks plus content creator recognition.

@Radiant Ritualist
Description: An extremely rare, golden-tier leadership role. Only awarded to true community leaders.
Perk: Highest community status, 5x nomination vote weight.

@Zealot
Description: Zealots are official Ritual Ambassadors. They are passionate community members who act as official representatives, bridging the gap between developers and the wider audience to promote growth, education, and adoption.
Perk: Ritual merchandise and judge roles during community events.

@Summoner
Description: An extremely rare and high-ranking role in the Ritual community. Very little public information is available about this role. Only two members are known to hold it: Thomas and FAE. Given how rare it is, it is considered one of the most prestigious roles in the ecosystem.
Perk: Unknown, but presumed to carry significant community authority.

SECTION 3: HOW TO UPGRADE YOUR ROLE
=============================================================

Question: How do I become a Bitty, Ritty, or Ritualist?
Answer: If you want to upgrade your role in the Ritual community, the best way is to consistently contribute and help the ecosystem grow.

According to @coffeedegen, there are three main ways to contribute to Ritual:

---

A. CONTRIBUTE BY EDUCATING OTHERS

Education is one of the most valuable ways to help the community because it makes Ritual easier to understand for both newcomers and existing members.

Ways to contribute through education:
1. Write clear articles, explanations, and guides about Ritual's technology, ecosystem, and ideas.
2. Create beginner-friendly tutorials that help new members understand how Ritual works step by step.
3. Break down difficult terms and technical concepts into simple explanations.
4. Summarize AMAs, Twitter Spaces, and Ritual Academy sessions for people who were not able to attend.
5. Answer questions from other members and help clarify misunderstandings.

When you educate others, you help make Ritual more accessible and easier to learn.

---

B. CONTRIBUTE BY CREATING CONTENT

Creative contributions help spread Ritual's identity and culture across the internet and make the community more engaging.

Ways to contribute through content creation:
1. Create Siggy fan art, profile pictures, banners, GIFs, stickers, or other visual assets.
2. Make Ritual-themed memes that reference real community moments, inside jokes, or ecosystem concepts.
3. Write lore pieces, storytelling content, or creative posts that expand the Ritual universe.
4. Share head canon ideas that add personality and imagination to the community narrative.
5. Post engaging social media content that highlights Ritual events, updates, and discussions.

Creative content helps Ritual reach more people while strengthening the community's identity.

---

C. CONTRIBUTE BY STRENGTHENING RITUAL'S CULTURE

A strong culture is what turns a group of users into a real community.

Ways to strengthen Ritual's culture:
1. Help newcomers patiently and respectfully when they ask questions.
2. Participate in games, events, discussions, and activities hosted by the community.
3. Encourage positive conversations and constructive engagement in the server.
4. Support community initiatives and collaborate with other members.
5. Report scammers, spammers, or suspicious behavior to moderators to keep the community safe.

=============================================================
SECTION 4: SUMMARY
=============================================================

To earn a role upgrade in Ritual, focus on consistent and meaningful contributions.
The more you educate, create, and support the community, the more you help Ritual grow and the better your chances of being recognized and promoted.

Role upgrades are not guaranteed by nominations alone. The final decision always rests with the Ritual team.
