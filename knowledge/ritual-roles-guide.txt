RITUAL COMMUNITY ROLES GUIDE

The main community roles in Ritual are: Ritty Bitty, Ritty, Ritualist, and Radiant Ritualist.

FULL ROLES LIST:

@Initiate - Newly verified members who have completed the server verification process. Perk: Welcome, basic community access.

@Ascendant - Members who have pledged to Ritual. This is the start of your community journey. Perk: Entry into community participation.

@Ritty Bitty - Early community members recognized for participation. On the right path, but with a long way to go. Perk: Access to the #ritual channel.

@Ritty - Long-term, loyal community members with conviction for what Ritual is building. Perk: Invited to an exclusive Telegram chat.

@Ritualist - The highest honor in the community. Awarded to those who have authentically demonstrated deep commitment to the project. Perk: Top-tier community recognition.

@Mage - A Ritualist with a specialization in content creation — art, writing, memes, and community growth. Perk: All Ritualist perks + content creator recognition.

@Radiant Ritualist - An extremely rare, golden-tier leadership role. Only awarded to true community leaders. Perk: Highest community status; 5x nomination vote weight.

@Forerunner - Members who were part of the Ritual community before its current phase. Perk: Legacy recognition.

HOW TO UPGRADE YOUR ROLE (How to become Ritty Bitty, Ritty, or Ritualist)

If you want to upgrade your role in the Ritual community, the best way is to consistently contribute and help the ecosystem grow.

According to @coffeedegen, there are three main ways you can contribute to Ritual:

A. Contribute by Educating Others
Education is one of the most valuable ways to help the community because it makes Ritual easier to understand for both newcomers and existing members.
Ways you can contribute include:
1. Writing clear articles, explanations, and guides about Ritual's technology, ecosystem, and ideas.
2. Creating beginner friendly tutorials that help new members understand how Ritual works step by step.
3. Breaking down difficult terms and technical concepts into simple explanations.
4. Summarizing AMAs, Twitter Spaces, and Ritual Academy sessions for people who were not able to attend.
5. Answering questions from other members and helping clarify misunderstandings.
When you educate others, you help make Ritual more accessible and easier to learn.

B. Contribute by Creating Content
Creative contributions help spread Ritual's identity and culture across the internet and make the community more engaging.
You can contribute through content such as:
1. Creating Siggy fan art, profile pictures, banners, GIFs, stickers, or other visual assets.
2. Making Ritual themed memes that reference real community moments, inside jokes, or ecosystem concepts.
3. Writing lore pieces, storytelling content, or creative posts that expand the Ritual universe.
4. Sharing head canon ideas that add personality and imagination to the community narrative.
5. Posting engaging social media content that highlights Ritual events, updates, and discussions.
Creative content helps Ritual reach more people while strengthening the community's identity.

C. Contribute by Strengthening Ritual's Culture
A strong culture is what turns a group of users into a real community.
You can help strengthen Ritual's culture by:
1. Helping newcomers patiently and respectfully when they ask questions.
2. Participating in games, events, discussions, and activities hosted by the community.
3. Encouraging positive conversations and constructive engagement in the server.
4. Supporting community initiatives and collaborating with other members.
5. Reporting scammers, spammers, or suspicious behavior to moderators to keep the community safe.

In short, consistent and meaningful contributions help you stand out and earn role upgrades. The more you educate, create, and support the community, the more you help Ritual grow.
